## Brand Tone Summary

**Feel:** Warm, professional, artisanal, community-centred
**Key values:** Quality ingredients, expert chefs, hands-on learning, inclusive
**Voice:** Inviting but confident. Hungarian warmth with culinary authority.
**What makes it distinctive:** It's not a restaurant — it's a place where cooking becomes an *experience*. The space itself (Carol Art&Cafe) signals that food, art, and community intersect here.
